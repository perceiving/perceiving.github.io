Is art subjective?

How do we explain the ascription of aesthetic value to mathematical proofs and conceptual art? Can we reconcile the insistence on first-hand perceptual experience in aesthetic judgement with the phenomenon of expertise? This 3-year project will improve our understanding of the relation between perception and cognition in philosophical aesthetics.
